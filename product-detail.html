The Story

Our House Blend represents the perfect introduction to specialty coffee. Combining the smooth, balanced sweetness of Colombian beans with the rich, full-bodied character of Brazilian coffee, this blend is designed to be your daily companion.

We source these beans from small-scale farmers who practice sustainable agriculture and traditional growing methods. The Colombian beans bring notes of caramel and citrus brightness, while the Brazilian coffee adds depth with its chocolatey undertones and nutty complexity.

Roasted to a medium profile, this blend strikes the ideal balance between origin characteristics and roast development. Whether you're brewing your first cup of the morning or savoring an afternoon break, House Blend delivers consistency and comfort in every sip.
